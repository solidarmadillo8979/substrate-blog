# Artificial Intelligence

Meanwhile, something new has begun appearing in the Substrate.

Artificial intelligence.

Systems trained to read information, make decisions, and help humans solve problems.

Companies like OpenAI and others are building these AI systems that live inside the digital world.

In the animated version, AI agents might look like explorers traveling across the network, navigating data streams and helping humans interact with the Substrate.

And above all of this…

Above the servers.
Above the cables.
Above the platforms.

Are humans.

The architects.
