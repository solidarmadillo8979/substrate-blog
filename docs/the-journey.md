# The Journey

Now imagine the camera zooms out.

We leave your phone screen…

…and suddenly we're inside a massive digital world.

Gigantic glowing highways stretch across the sky.
Rivers of blue light rush through deep data canyons.
Packets of information zoom past like tiny comets.

This isn't fantasy.

This is what data actually does.

Every time someone sends a message…
posts a video…
plays a game…
or asks an AI a question…

tiny bundles of information called data packets race through networks across the planet.

In the Substrate, these packets would look like little glowing travelers, flying through the digital landscape carrying pieces of human thought.
