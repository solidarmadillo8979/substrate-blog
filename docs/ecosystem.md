# The Ecosystem

Now here's where the story gets interesting.

The Substrate isn't just roads and machines.

It's an ecosystem.

Some systems protect the network like giant digital guardians.

Companies like Cloudflare run massive defense networks that block attacks and keep websites safe.

In the animated world, they'd look like towering firewall fortresses projecting shields across the digital sky.

But not everything in the Substrate is friendly.

Hidden in the shadows are malware creatures.

Viruses made of corrupted code.
Bots swarming like robotic insects.
Hackers sending digital predators into the network.

They roam the landscape looking for weaknesses.

If the Substrate were a show, they'd be the monsters lurking in the dark corners of the system.
