# Underwater Cables

But wait.

There's something even bigger connecting everything together.

Under the oceans of the real world are giant fiber-optic cables stretching thousands of miles between continents.

These cables carry light signals that move data across the globe at incredible speeds.

In the Substrate world, these would look like giant glowing highways under a digital ocean, pulsing with energy as messages travel from continent to continent.

Your meme from Houston might fly through cables to California… then bounce to Europe… then back again… all in seconds.
